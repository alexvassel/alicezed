Tchaikovsky

This project is an information website, which aim is to acquaint visitor with the works of Tchaikovsky and especially his cycle "The Seasons". The site disclose some details about the biography of the composer and about the history of the creation of the cycle and publication of it. Also the visitor can download the musical notes of the cycle.

A main page and the page named "The seasons" have their own quick search. That looks like a local links in the left column of the site. Site has a photographic material that also allows visually perceive the information.

Site has a simple tree structure: it consists of the main page with a menu in which you can go to the pages of the second level. They have the same structure as the main page with changes in design and color scheme in accordance to the content.

The menu is made by using the html-tag "canvas" and the programming language JavaScript. The background is made programmatically as smooth gradient from top to bottom.

The design was developed for each season separately, depending on the standard color palette of months. Winter is done in pale shades of blue in according with the header, in which is used a photo of the St. Petersburg during the winter. Spring was made with the usage of green color. There is a photo of a blooming tree in header, which is typical for this time of year.

In the process of creating the project have been used programs such as Adobe Photoshop CS3 to edit graphics, Adobe Dreamweaver CS3 to create a site in the languages ​​of html, css and JavaScript and Adobe Flash CS3 to create a frame-based animation, which serves as an introduction and entrance to the site. JavaScript has been used for the programming such elements as checker box, radio-buttons, "canvas", "audio", range component and also a loading of xml-files.

Summarizing I can say that the project includes the following information:

  • xml-file (menu);
  • Flash components (Action Script 3.0 menu / graphics);
  • Canvas component (menu);
  • External css files;
  • Range components with using the html 5 (volume range and resizing of /old/images);
  • xml-file with the contents of the site (gallery of autumn);
  • Audio player with buttons stop / play, choice of music;
  • Video player with a podcast on the site;
  • html 5 layout (body, header, nav, footer);
  • Recognition of the browser for support in Safari, Opera, Chrome, Firefox;
  • Gallery with a support for opening picture in a new window / new layer;
  • Radio-buttons;
  • Checker box-buttons (normal version of the site / print version);
  • Support changes in css.

The only thing that does not match is a font. Grunge fonts are fonts that are spread out around the edges, sloppy, inaccurate, like avant-garde posters of the end of 60s, i.e. until the advent of digital printing. In the case of this project - it is an individually selected font to the entire site that is embedded in it through the JavaScript and html-5 technologies. After there were added the numerous coffee and watercolor spots, two cups of coffee, coffee beans and spray paints. So was born the grunge on the site.

Do you know that..?

This is my first application for the iPhone or iPad, which allows users to know smth interesting about the world.

About what?

Don't know what to do with yourself? Do you tired of reading news and boring books? Don't talk about? Do your life seems gray, boring and depressing? And do you want something hot, interesting and exciting? If at least on one of these questions you answered "yes", then this application is created specifically for you. A huge number of interesting, strange, shocking and amazing facts from around the world are waiting for you. One click and all the wonderful news close to you immediately. More than that on the pages of this application you can find not only beautiful pictures for the each articles, but even a thematic music.

Why?

You ask me: "Why exactly this application? Why it is not about space, fashion, dogs or cats, politics, why not about movies or the main characters of your favorite book? Why it is not schedule of electric trains or maps of cities?" The idea of creation the application "Do you know that..?" was born suddenly and almost randomly. Just one day I went through the metro station "Revolution Square" and looked at how people stroke muzzles of dogs. Immediately I remembered that it is to good luck. And I was wondering what other interesting facts live around us.

You ask me: "Why exactly application? Why I can not find an interesting information via the Internet?" I answer you: "Because you need to find. Because you want to read, but not straining or not looking".

You ask me: "Why the application has exactly such design?" So, the design is made in the best traditions of the company Apple. Here an 'apple' theme was applied for it, but some colors and background image were changed to create an individual image. The font has remained as standard - Helvetica. All of the shadows, gradients, borders, buttons, arrows have not been changed too.

You ask me: "Why blue?" Blue is the color of sky and sea, religions, art, music, peace, compassion and peace. It is the color of constancy and perseverance. The application is aimed at harmony with others, to the knowledge of the outside world, so the shades of blue are perfect here together, they are in harmony, do not cause irritability and tension. By the way this is the reason why many well-known companies are using this color as the main.

You ask me: "Why exactly this logo?" Logo was created for a long time. It has been considered many options, including a hat magician, the human brain, monkey, people who share information, dialogue, different variations of question marks. All that associated with a mystery and a suspense. But the choice was made for a person with balloons in hand, which personify here the questions. A person finds answers to his questions and releases them from himself.

For whom?

You ask me: "For whom is this application?" The most interesting fact is that this application will be interesting for everyone. You can not know everything, and now a one of the most important needs of modern person is to learn something new. Therefore the target audience is very different: the application will be perfect for both children and adults, both men and women. It does not matter either education or social status, or marital status or place of residence.

How?

You ask me: "How to use the application?" The application "Do you know that..?" Application is optimized for the iPhone 3g device, but it can also be easily viewed both on the iPhone 4 and the iPad, that allows considerably expand the potential audience.

You ask me: "How it was done?" In the process of creating an application have been used such programs as Adobe Photoshop CS5 to edit all the /old/images that illustrate the content of each article and to create a seamless texture. Espresso program to create the application itself (html5, css3, JavaScript, jQuery), Adobe Illustrator CS5 program for creating and designing a logo and also such programs as Adobe Device Central CS5 and xCode to test applications on different devices. xCode is a program that was used not only for emulation iPad and iPhone devices, but also to connect the real device to the computer for further testing directly on it.

You ask me: "How separate the articles?" The application consists of the links, which are separated into categories. People used to categorize everything, because a sort clearly separates one from another, making it easy to find the most relevant and interesting article. More than that every article contains a text and a picture. It helps visually interpret the information and allows to learn it better.

Advertising

After the development of corporate identity and creation the site, it was the task to create advertising. So I decided that the main advantage is durability of the goods, then the ads had been to reflect exactly this property primarily.

The purpose of developing

Declare about the company "Watchman", to focus consumers on the positive quality of the goods - durability.

Disadvantages

In the magazine: consumers often ignore these ads. In this sense television and radio advertising will be more effective. Moreover, readers of magazines, as a rule, mature individuals who are changing their preferences to certain goods with less desire than audiences of other media. Yet sometimes the quality of printed text is very low because of what lose the quality of photos, text, may disappear entirely a few details.

In the billboards: people have only a few seconds to digest the information and it is constantly exposed to weathering and vandalism.

Idea

Match the word «durability» with a short-lived human life. To do this, was selected the image of the skeleton of a person with an intact watch on his arm. As another example: compare the word «durability» contrary to the strength and durability. Therefore it was chosen a picture of the sculpture with a clock on the hand. This approach helps the consumer to ensure a positive quality of the product on a comparative basis.

As another example, advertising in the magazine as a comic book where a person in an amusement park, playing, trying to break the clock with a hammer, but in the end he realizes that it is impossible, the clock remained intact. In my opinion, this is a good example of quality goods:

The purpose of the advertising campaign

To expand the range of consumers.

Slogan

The slogan is very simple and consists of one word - durability, which means strength, endurance, hard work. This slogan is short and understandable, and most importantly it immediately points consumers on the advantage of goods. Slogan supported by illustrations that clearly demonstrate this quality.

Conclusion

The advertising campaign is to attract the attention of consumers, to increase their number, and finally to increase the number of sales. The main condition was to focus the buyer on the durability, quality and efficiency of goods, and also on the corporate identity (logo, colors).
